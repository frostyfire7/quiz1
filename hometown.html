Semarang

The capital of Central Java province, Indonesia as well as the fifth largest metropolitan city in Indonesia after Jakarta, Surabaya, Bandung and Medan.

As one of the most developed in Java, Semarang city had a total population of nearly 2 million people during the day and could reach 2.5 million.

City of Semarang was headed by Mayor Hendrar Prihadi.

Semarang also known as historical city because of its history and many old building still exist until nowadays.

There are lot of delicious local food and incredible tourist destinations in Semarang.
